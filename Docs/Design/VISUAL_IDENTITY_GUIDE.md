## Part 6: Asset Specifications

### 3D Model Standards

#### Scooter Models
- **LOD System**: 
  - LOD0: Full detail, high-poly (100k+ tris) - showcase/customize screen
  - LOD1: Medium detail (30-50k tris) - garage/preview
  - LOD2: Low detail (10-15k tris) - racing view
  - LOD3: Ultra-low (3-5k tris) - distance/multiplayer

- **Texture Resolution**:
  - Showcase: 4K PBR textures (Diffuse, Normal, Roughness, Metallic, AO)
  - Gameplay: 2K PBR textures
  - UI: 1K compressed

#### Part Models
- **Modularity**: Each part should cleanly attach to mounting points
- **Poly Count**: 
  - High-detail parts: 5-10k tris
  - Medium parts: 2-5k tris
  - Simple parts: 500-2k tris
- **Pivot Points**: Consistent positioning for rotational preview

### Material System

#### PBR Material Structure
Each surface should have:
1. **Albedo/Diffuse Map**: Base color, no lighting
2. **Normal Map**: Surface detail (4K or 2K)
3. **Roughness Map**: Shine variation
4. **Metallic Map**: Metal vs. non-metal identification
5. **Ambient Occlusion**: Crevice shadows
6. **Detail Map**: Micro-texture overlays (wear, dirt, scratches)

#### Special Effects
- **Paint Layers**: Clearcoat with specular highlights
- **Decal System**: Unique decal material with transparency
- **Weathering**: Procedural dirt, dust, grease effects
- **Neon/Glow**: Emissive materials for custom neon accents

### Texture Atlasing
- Parts grouped by type for efficient rendering
- Shared materials where possible
- Atlas size: 2048x2048 for gameplay, 4096x4096 for showcase

## Part 7: UI/UX Visual Language

### Design Principles
- **Industrial/Workshop Aesthetic**: Metal, bolts, stickers, grease
- **Parts Catalog Style**: Clean labels, technical diagrams
- **Tactile Interface**: Buttons feel physical, interactive

### Color Scheme for UI
- **Background**: Dark charcoal (#1a1a1a), neutral warm grays
- **Text**: Bright white for primary, light gray for secondary
- **Accents**: Brand-appropriate colors (cyan for KRONOS, red for VELOCE, orange for THRUST)
- **Warnings/Status**: 
  - Green: Good condition
  - Yellow: Caution (worn)
  - Red: Critical (damaged)
  - Blue: Information

### Component Styling
- **Buttons**: Metallic beveled edges, material shadows
- **Cards**: Dark background with brand-color accent bar or border
- **Icons**: Monoline technical style, brand-consistent
- **Typography**: Geometric, modern sans-serif (Roboto, Interstellar, or similar)
- **Visual Feedback**: Glow, scale, rotation animations on interaction

### Overlay Elements
- **Part Rarity Badge**: Corner-mounted, color-coded glow
- **Stats Display**: Numeric values with visual bars (progress style)
- **Comparison UI**: Side-by-side stat visualization
- **Part Inspector**: 3D rotation view with measurement callouts
